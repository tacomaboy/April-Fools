Major Announcement

Goodbye Electrons.
Hello Gasoline.

After years of quiet reflection, deep market analysis, and one very long board meeting, Rivian has made a decision that will reshape the automotive industry — again.

Starting Q1 2026, all new Rivian R1T and R1S vehicles will be available in a new Gas Edition trim, powered by a choice of naturally aspirated V8 or turbocharged V6 engines. Existing electric vehicles will continue to be supported through... well, definitely through next quarter.

The all-new R1T Gas Edition delivers an estimated 18 MPG highway — a number our engineers describe as "a starting point." The R1S Gas Edition seats 7 adults and 1 standard-size gas can stored conveniently in the frunk.

What's New

Reimagined for the
Gasoline Era

⛽
Gas Tunnel™ Storage
Replacing the Gear Tunnel, the new Gas Tunnel holds up to 4 standard fuel canisters, because you never know when you'll need more. Designed for the adventurer who plans ahead.
🔊
V8 Adventure Sound
Finally. The roar you've been secretly craving. Our 5.0L naturally aspirated V8 produces a soundtrack that turns heads, wakes neighbors, and alerts wildlife. It's a feature, not a bug.
🗺️
Gas Station Navigation
Our revamped trip planner now routes you through scenic gas stations coast to coast. Stop more, see more. Each fill-up is an opportunity to discover a new beef jerky flavor.
🛢️
ExxonMobil Partnership
We've partnered with ExxonMobil to bring the "Rivian Fuel Experience" to 4,000 select gas stations nationwide. Expect exclusive Rivian pump handles, branded receipts, and mild disappointment.
🌬️
Exhaust Mode™
Choose from three exhaust profiles: Whisper (it's still loud), Sport (louder), and Maximum Adventure (legally discouraged in 12 states). Your drive, your expression.
📉
Simplified Range Anxiety
No more worrying about charge levels. Just gas! Available at 150,000 stations nationwide. We've traded kilowatt-hours for gallons. Net change in anxiety: unclear.
The future of adventure isn't about electron flow — it's about horsepower, raw torque, and the undeniable satisfaction of a full tank. We're going back to basics, and frankly, our accountants are thrilled.
RJ Scaringe
Founder & CEO, Rivian Automotive
